Fried Dumpling

These authentic Jamaican fried dumplings are the real deal! They’re perfectly puffy, golden brown on the outside.

Орц

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Хийх дараалал

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, eggs, and melted butter; whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each dumpling and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with honey, fruits, or syrup.
